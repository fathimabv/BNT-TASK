Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

"Missile Man of India"

About

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Known for his humble personality and deep wisdom, he played a crucial role in India's civilian space and military missile programs.

Achievements

  • Worked as a scientist at ISRO and DRDO.
  • Played a key role in India’s Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Served as the 11th President of India (2002–2007).
  • Authored several inspirational books like "Wings of Fire".
  • Awarded Bharat Ratna in 1997.

Legacy

Dr. Kalam inspired millions of students and young Indians with his speeches and writings. He believed in dreaming big and working hard to achieve it. He passed away in 2015 while doing what he loved—teaching.
